Bongo and Beanie are best palls, and they just love to play on the see-saw. Help them play their favorite game by lifting Bongo up to the right height, then dropping him on the see-saw to send Beanie flying into the pool!

Click-and-drag to lift, let go to drop - the further Bongo falls, the further Beanie flies. Land Beanie in the pool three times to complete the game.   

This game uses the “click, drag, and drop” mouse technique, along with basic spatial reasoning. Players work out the relationship between the height of Bongo’s drop and the distance of Beanie’s flight, then use their understanding of this relationship to hit their target. The concepts of trajectory and force are implicitly introduced.
