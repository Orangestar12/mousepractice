Everyone loves to pop bubbles – and Peter Pickle is no exception! While out on a picnic (or maybe a picklenick!) with his dad, this pickle needs your help to pop each and every one of those bubbles.

Use the mouse to move the pickle back and forth and click to make him jump. Each round, pop every bubble and you get a star. Win 5 rounds in a row and you win! But make sure you don’t miss any!
