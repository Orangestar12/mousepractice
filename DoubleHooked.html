Mama Octopus is the best fisher on the lake. With her eight arms and her big brain, she can keep track of two fishing lines at once! Help Mama Octopus lower her fishing hooks to the right depth so that she can catch some tasty fish.

Think fast so they don’t get away! Look out for sharks, though…catching one of them can make you lose your stars!    

Basic spatial reasoning skills are used here, including judging heights and distances. The game also requires some multitasking ability, as the players will need to keep track of two fishing lines at once – guiding them toward goals and keeping them away from hazards. This game is best for kids who understand the basics of clicking and cursor-control.
