Orwell is a very special little pig. Using his springy tail and his cardboard wings, he can do what most pigs only dream about: he can fly! Help guide Orwell to the flavorful flying fruits by bouncing him from cloud to cloud.

Click to get him started, then use the mouse to control his flight. But be careful…Orwell’s wings don’t work too well, and if he doesn’t land on a cloud he’ll take quite a tumble! Collect five snacks without falling down to complete the game.    

In this game, players use basic cursor control, timing, and hand-eye coordination to move their character up through the clouds. Accurate judgement of simulated distances and trajectories is essential for success, as is hand-eye coordination. Children also use their planning skills to determine which snacks Orwell can get without falling, and which ones he must leave behind.
