It’s a hot summer day on the pond and the air is buzzing with flies – good news for a hungry frog! Freddie is especially hungry and wants to eat every last one of those big, juicy bugs.

Use the cursor to aim and click to make him shoot out his tongue. Any flies in the path of Freddie’s tongue will be eaten. Lick up every last fly to win!   

Simple point-and-click mouse skills are utilized in this game. Players move the cursor into the most favorable spot and time their clicking so that Freddie’s tongue intersects with the flies’ flight paths. Basic timing skills and judgement of spatial relationships are reinforced.
